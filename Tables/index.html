Description :

India is known for its rich cultural heritage. Diversification is the identity of the country. Indian dance (classical dance of India) is one of the most revered identities of our culture. In India, dance forms can be broadly classified into 2 categories classical and folk dance forms.

Bharatnatyam is the oldest dance form in India.
